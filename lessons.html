LingoCity Classroom Lessons are designed to supplement your language learning journey, by providing you with practice, insight and useful information about Korea at the level you feel most comfortable with. We currently have three main levels:

 

Beginner Practice Videos

We help you using English instruction from the Korean alphabet to having everyday conversations. A great way to apply your knowledge in a cutrually appropriate manner.

Dictation Videos

We prompt you with Korean Sentences, and you need to write what you hear (without looking at the sentence). We then show you the correct answer, along with a translation. A great way to practice your listening!
