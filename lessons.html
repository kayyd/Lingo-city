Classroom Lessons

LingoCity Classroom Lessons are a collection of the best visual aids to supplement your language learning journey. We support you by providing you with practice, insight and useful information about Korea at the level you feel most comfortable with. We currently have two levels: Getting Started and Beginnger. We also have a final set of videos looking specifically at Korean signs for the more advanced linguists visiting us here! We hope to improve of this and include intermediate and advanced materials in the future.

 

Getting Started
